## 1. Your Responsibility

You are fully responsible for the applications you build on the Services and for the content and behavior of those applications. Even when calling models through BearLab, you remain the **service provider** to your own end users.

## 2. Compliance Obligations

- **Content compliance**: ensure content your application generates or displays meets applicable legal requirements.
- **User protection**: protect your users' data and comply with applicable privacy laws (e.g. GDPR, CCPA).
- **Data security**: take reasonable measures to secure your systems and user data.
- **AI disclosure**: where required by applicable regulations, disclose to users that content is AI-generated.
- **Provider terms**: comply with the terms of the underlying Third-Party Model Providers.

## 3. Prohibited Uses

The following are prohibited (non-exhaustive):

- Generating or distributing unlawful, harmful, or infringing content
- Fraud, phishing, malware, or other malicious activity
- Large-scale spam or automated abuse
- Attempts to bypass rate limits, security controls, or usage restrictions
- Unauthorized resale or proxying of the Services (see Terms §4.6)
- Use in high-risk contexts requiring fail-safe performance
- Activity violating export control, sanctions, or anti-money-laundering laws
