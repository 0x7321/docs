title: "User Levels"
description: "The difference between Tier 1 and Tier 2, and how to upgrade."
---

The platform has two user levels, mainly affecting [rate limits](/en/guides/rate-limits) and discounts.

| Tier | How to get it | Rate limit |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Tier 1** | Default after registration | 5 RPM |
| **Tier 2** | Automatic after any top-up | No limit |

## Tier 1

All new accounts start at Tier 1, limited to **5 RPM** (5 requests per minute), suitable for light testing.

## Tier 2

After **any top-up**, your account is automatically upgraded to Tier 2 with no rate limit. The upgrade is immediate, with no manual request. Higher levels may also receive price discounts; see [Pricing](/en/guides/pricing).

## Check your level

Sign in and go to Dashboard, Profile Settings to view your current level.
