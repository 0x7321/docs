Pay only for what you use. No subscription, no minimums — every request is metered against your account balance.

## How billing works

Your account holds a **credit balance** measured in BearLab AI quota. Every request deducts quota based on the model you call and how many tokens it processes (or, for some models, a flat per-call price). USD amounts shown throughout the console use a fixed conversion:

<Note>
**500,000 quota = 1 USD.** All per-token and per-call figures on this page are expressed in USD for readability.
</Note>

Your final cost for any request is also scaled by the **group multiplier** of the [provider group](/en/guides/providers) the request is routed through, and [user levels](/en/guides/user-levels) may apply additional discounts.

## Two billing methods

Each model is billed in one of two ways, decided by its `quota_type`:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pay per token" icon="coins">
    `quota_type = 0`. Input and output tokens are priced separately. This covers the conversational/chat models — Claude, GPT-5.x, Gemini, Grok, and the domestic models.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pay per call" icon="receipt">
    `quota_type = 1`. A flat price is charged per successful request regardless of token count. Used for image generation, CLI/per-request packages, and metered endpoints.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Pay-per-token pricing

For token-billed models, BearLab AI uses three numbers:

| Field | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `model_ratio` | Input price multiplier. **Input USD per 1M tokens = `model_ratio` × 2.** |
| `completion_ratio` | Output multiplier relative to input. **Output USD per 1M tokens = input price × `completion_ratio`.** |
| `cache_ratio` | Cache-hit input tokens are billed at `cache_ratio` of the input price (most chat models: `0.1`, i.e. a 90% discount). See [prompt caching](/en/guides/prompt-caching). |

Token counts come from `prompt_tokens` and `completion_tokens` in the response `usage` object.

<Note>
**Cost formula (token-billed):**
`cost = (input_tokens × input_price + output_tokens × input_price × completion_ratio) × group_ratio`
where `input_price = model_ratio × $2 / 1M tokens`. Cached input tokens substitute `model_ratio × cache_ratio`.
</Note>

### Tiered / context-length pricing

Some models — primarily the domestic models routed through Alibaba Cloud (DeepSeek, Qwen, GLM, Kimi, MiniMax) — use a **tiered expression**: the per-token rate changes with the input length of the request. For example, longer-context Qwen and GLM tiers charge a higher input multiplier above 32K / 128K / 256K tokens. These models display their tier breakpoints on the console Pricing page; the billed rate is selected automatically from the request's input length.

## Pay-per-call pricing

For `quota_type = 1` models, a fixed `model_price` (USD) is charged per successful request. `model_ratio` and `completion_ratio` do not apply. The group multiplier still applies.

| Category | Example models | Per-call price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Image generation | `gpt-image-2` | $0.05 |
| Image generation (VIP) | `gpt-image-2-vip` | $0.065 |
| Image generation | `gemini-3-pro-image-preview` | $0.15 |
| Gemini CLI / per-request | `gemini-2.5-pro-cli`, `gemini-3.1-pro-preview-cli` | $0.0001 |
| Gemini metered tiers | `gemini-3-pro-preview-c1`, `gemini-2.5-pro-c1` | $0.0002 – $0.0003 |
| Kiro-Claude per-request | `kiro-claude-opus-4-8`, `kiro-claude-sonnet-4-6` | $0.000305 – $0.00058 |

<Note>
Per-call cost is simply `model_price × group_ratio`. A failed or rejected request is not billed.
</Note>

## Free models

If your key resolves only to the **free group**, the models you see (for example `claude-opus-4-7`, `deepseek-v4-pro`, `gpt-5.4-mini`, `claude-opus-4-8`) are routed at no cost to your balance. The console may show a nominal `model_price`, but the effective group multiplier for these models is **0**, so they do not draw down quota. See the [models guide](/en/guides/models) for the full free-model list returned by your key.

## Group multipliers and discounts

The same model can carry a different effective price depending on the [provider group](/en/guides/providers) your key is bound to. The price you pay equals the model's base price (token or per-call) times the group multiplier. Higher [user levels](/en/guides/user-levels) may unlock additional discounts.

## Top-up

- Top up your balance from the console; the credited quota is available immediately.
- For enterprise billing or large top-ups, [contact us](mailto:support@bearlab.ai).

## Balance alerts

To avoid interruptions, set a low-balance threshold in the console under **Profile → Settings**. When your balance drops below it, we notify you by email or in-app message.

## High-volume plans

For higher concurrency or dedicated pricing, [contact us](mailto:support@bearlab.ai).

## Refunds

To request a refund, contact support. Note:

- If a refund drops your account level, the over-benefit amount is deducted.
- Top-ups for which an invoice was issued are non-refundable.
- A processing fee of up to **5%** applies.
- Refunds return to the original payment method.

For a formal invoice, contact support with your company name, tax ID, and amount.
