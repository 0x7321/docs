Pay only for what you use. No minimum, and it scales elastically with your business.

## Billing currency

The platform bills in **USD**, aligned with each provider's official pricing.

## Billing method

Conversational models are billed **per token**: input and output tokens are priced separately, and input tokens that hit [prompt caching](/en/guides/prompt-caching) get a lower rate.

Token usage per request is taken from `prompt_tokens` and `completion_tokens` in the response `usage`.

## Provider multipliers and discounts

Each [provider](/en/guides/providers) has its own **price multiplier**. The price you pay equals the model base price times the provider multiplier. Higher [user levels](/en/guides/user-levels) may receive additional discounts.

## Top-up

- Online top-up via **Stripe** (credit and debit cards); balance is credited instantly.
- For enterprise billing or large top-ups, [contact us](mailto:support@bearlab.ai).

## Balance alerts

To avoid interruptions, set an alert: Dashboard, Profile Settings, Subscription Management, set a threshold. When your balance drops below it, we notify you by email or in-app message.

## High-volume plans

For higher concurrency or dedicated pricing, [contact us](mailto:support@bearlab.ai).

## Refunds

To request a refund, contact support. Note:

- If a refund drops your account level, the over-benefit amount is deducted.
- Top-ups for which an invoice was issued are non-refundable.
- A processing fee of up to **5%** applies.
- Refunds return to the original payment method.

Stripe emails a receipt after each successful top-up. For a formal invoice, contact support with your company name, tax ID, and amount.
